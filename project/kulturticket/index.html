Kulturticket Konstanz

Aug 9, 2020
Searchwing Logo

The Kulturticket Konstanz allows all students to enter the theatre and philharmonic concerts without charge. This can be realized by paying a small fee of 1.30 EUR per student per semester to both institutions and includes any event including premieres. Students simply have to show up at the cashier an hour before the event and will get the best remaining tickets for free. Additionally the Zebra Kino, KULA and many other places regularly offer reduced prices or free entries subsidized through the ticket.

Making this ticket a reality was probably the highlight of my carrer as a student representative. For the price of 2 beers over a whole bachelors students can visit concerts and plays for free. I myself used this offering a lot and I hope it will give first semesters a feeling of being welome in the city.
