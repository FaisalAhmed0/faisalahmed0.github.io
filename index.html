I am a teaching assistant at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences and a guest researcher at the Max Planck Institute For Intelligent Systems. I am interested in reinforcement learning (RL), specifically, unsupervised RL unsupervised RL and representation learning. At some time after my master, I was lucky to be mentored by Ben Eysenbach and Claudio Michaelis.I spent sometime at the University of Tuebingen as part of the AIMS-Tübingen Fellowship Program Data Science & AI
My long-term research goal is to develop generalist agents, agents capable of understanding the world around them by acting on it, to achieve this goal I focus my research on deep reinforcement learning, unsupervised learning, and representation learning.
